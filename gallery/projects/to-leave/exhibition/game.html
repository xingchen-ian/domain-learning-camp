To Leave

A game about packing, leaving, and what we carry with us.

How to Play

  1. Read the clues left in the room — they hint at who this person really is.
  2. Click any object to inspect it. Some objects matter more than they seem.
  3. Enter Memory on an object to feel why it matters to this person.
  4. Pack what feels right — balance what they need with what they love.
  5. Finish Packing when done. You'll see how well you understood them.

Clues and memories are your real tools. Trust your reading of the person — not numbers.

Understanding the character will help you pack what they truly need.

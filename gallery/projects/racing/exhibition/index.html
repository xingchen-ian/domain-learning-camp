Racing

A top-down police chase racing game — outrun the cops, manage your fuel, and survive the road.

Play the Game

Game Idea

Goal

Reach the destination before time runs out — while being chased by police, managing your fuel, avoiding traffic damage, and learning when to speed up and slow down.

Core Learning Shift

Beginners think they can just speed away and drive recklessly, but experts know sometimes you have to slow down and have control. This game teaches resource management under pressure: speed drains gas, damage slows you down, and accelerating blindly makes you an easy target for traffic and the cops.

Domain Knowledge

The Real-World Domain

Car racing (and getaway driving) is about more than just speed. Real drivers must balance acceleration with control, monitor vehicle health, and manage fuel consumption — especially under pursuit.

Novice Misconception

Beginners assume the car won't get damaged when it crashes, that fuel is unlimited, and that constant acceleration is the answer. They don't use the brake and think faster always means better.

Expert Judgment

Experts understand that sometimes you need to slow down to find the right gap, that fuel must be optimized, that damage compounds, and that a controlled escape is better than a reckless one. They read the road, the sirens, and the dashboard.

Why This Becomes Playable

By making gas, health, police distance, and boost energy visible and interactive, the game forces players to experience the tradeoffs experts face — and learn through repeated play that control beats chaos.

System Design

Player Learning Loop

Observe surrounding traffic, car speed, health, boost energy, police distance

Judge whether you can fit through gaps, whether police are too close, whether gas is low

Act accelerate, brake, steer, boost, collect gas tokens

Read Feedback sirens get louder, dashboard lights flash, damage slows the car

Adjust speed up when sirens are loud, slow down to navigate gaps, refuel when gas is low

Environment Data

Road & lanes Traffic cars Police car Gas tokens Time limit Distance to destination Car health Boost energy Siren proximity

Player-Controlled Data

Accelerate / Brake Steer left / right Boost (space) Collect gas tokens

System-Calculated Results

Police distance → siren volume Damage → slower speed + faster gas drain Speed + boost → gas drain rate

Feedback Translation

Siren gets louder
Police is getting closer. Background flashes red and blue.
Gas is low
Alert sound plays. Collect gas tokens on the road.
Car is damaged
Slower top speed, faster gas drain, smoke from engine.

Success & Failure

Win: Reach the destination in time

Lose: Time runs out Lose: Police catches you Lose: Run out of fuel Lose: Car destroyed

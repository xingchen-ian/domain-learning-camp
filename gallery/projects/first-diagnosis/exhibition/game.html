Move

WASD or arrow keys to walk through the clearing. Trees, the jet, and fire block your path.

2

Find

Some patients are obvious; others are hidden behind the jet or in the trees. Walk close to reveal them.

3

Search

Equipment is inside and around the jet. Casts, big bandages, cloths, and water must be picked up before they can be used.

4

Treat

Walk up to a found patient and press E (or click). The world keeps running — act fast, but think first.

5

Stabilize

Match the right tool to the right injury. Clean before stitch. Use a cast for a fracture. Don't over-treat.

6

Adjust

If a patient loses three others, the mission ends. Read the body — bleeding, popping stitches, swelling — and try a different plan.

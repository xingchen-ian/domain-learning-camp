Choose Your Challenge

Each level changes wind, obstacles, and energy — not just visuals.

Controls

W / ↑ Ascend (uses energy)
S / ↓ Dive (regenerates energy)
1 🐦 Sparrow wing (light, efficient)
2 🐦 Bluebird wing (balanced)
3 🦅 Eagle wing (powerful, energy-hungry)
Space Boost flap
R Restart level
Esc Back to menu
🐟 Fish mode (Level 4): Fly through the dive portal to transform! Same W/↑/S/↓ controls swim underwater. Auto-transforms back after 10s.

Core learning: Don't just fly straight. Observe the wind vane, manage your energy by diving to regen, and plan your route around storms.

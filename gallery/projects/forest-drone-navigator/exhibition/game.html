Forest Drone Navigator · 3D

Fly your drone through the forest and reach the LAND pad with altitude at 0 to win.
Watch out for building-tall trees (fly over) and overhead cables (dip under), and always compensate for the wind.

Tip: Inside a level, press Space to see the controls and start.

Controls (press Space to start / pause)

W Throttle forward
S Brake / reverse
A Strafe left
D Strafe right
U Ascend
H Descend
Ctrl Boost: hold Control for extra thrust (faster, but more inertia—ease off early)
Space Pause / resume (shows this guide)

🎯 Win: Fly to the LAND pad at the end and hold H to bring altitude down to 0. Touch down off the pad and you crash.

🌬️ The WIND readout shows speed and direction; the wind keeps pushing you off course—compensate against it early.

🌲 Fly over tall trees; ⌁ stay below the marked height to pass under overhead cables.

⏱ The timer in the top-right counts down; harder tiers have less time (P1 60s / P2 40s / P3 30s). Hit zero and the mission fails.

⚠️ Advanced (P2 / P3): Stronger wind here, and some trees rise above your 70 m start height—you must actively climb over them. Cables force you to drop low and slip beneath. Read the wind first, then weave an S-shaped path switching between 'climb over trees / dive under cables'.

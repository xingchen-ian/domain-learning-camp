In the Opera House

The final curtain has fallen and the audience has gone home — yet the opera singer remains inside the great opera house, locked in by mistake as the stagehands bolted the heavy doors for the night. The corridors are dark, the chandeliers dim, and no one is coming to let her out.

Your mission: guide the opera singer from the dressing room out onto the stage. Uncover the hidden clues, mend the broken spotlight, and find the golden key that opens the side door — then help her escape before the theatre is sealed for good.

🐱 Raise a Cat Successfully

A cat-keeping simulation game by Siyi Mo

Core Learning Shift:
Beginners think pet keeping means just feeding some food and having fun. Experts know it means changing their methods depending on their pets' physical condition and the different characters of each cat.
▶ Play the Game

🎯 Player Goal

Keep your cat healthy and happy for at least 15 years. Feed it the right food, play with it, keep it clean, and take it to the vet when needed.

🐱 Three Different Cats

Each cat has a unique personality — a balanced house cat, a formerly stray rescue, and a hyperactive kitten. Your strategy must adapt to each one.

📊 Observe & Adjust

Watch health, affection, fullness, energy, water, and cleanliness levels. Read the cat's behavior signals and adjust your care accordingly.

⚠️ Real Stakes

Too much or too little food can harm your cat. Ignoring the cat leads to depression. Missing illness signs can be fatal. Just like real pet keeping.

💡 Learn by Doing

The game teaches through feedback: your cat's tail flicks when happy, the room dims when it's sick, it meows when hungry. Learn to read the signals.

🔧 Simple to Run

No installation needed. The game runs in any modern browser with no internet connection required. Just click and play.

Quick Start

  1. Click "Play the Game" to go to the game page.
  2. Choose one of three cats: Mimi (balanced), Shadow (formerly stray), or Bounce (high energy).
  3. Watch the cat's status bars (Health, Affection, Fullness, Energy, Water, Cleanliness).
  4. Use the action buttons to feed, water, play, wash, and care for your cat.
  5. Pay attention to the cat's behavior: meowing (hungry), dim light (sick), glow (wants to play).
  6. Keep your cat alive and happy until it reaches 15 years old to win!

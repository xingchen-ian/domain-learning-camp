Domain Knowledge: Cat Keeping

Real-World Experience

I have four cats at home, and one of them was discovered homeless on the street. It's really difficult to keep pets healthy, especially when raising a previously homeless cat — it depends on various factors like feeding, caring, medical examining, and illness preventing.

Novice Misconception: Many people keep pets on impulse without thinking about costs and time. They often feed pets too much food and ignore accompanying them. They always interact with cats regardless of their character and health condition.
Expert Judgment: The most important domain challenge is the owner's judgement on the food they feed the cat and understanding the different cats' characters. Experts change their methods depending on the cat's physical condition and personality — they don't treat all cats the same way.

Why This Knowledge Becomes Playable

In the game, players must observe each cat's stats and behavior, judge what the cat needs, act by choosing the right care action, read feedback from the cat's reactions, and adjust their strategy. Different cat personalities require different approaches — exactly like real pet keeping.

Player-Controlled Data (Actions)

ActionEffect
Add Food / Reduce FoodAdjusts food amount in bowl. Choose food type: Dry, Wet, or Special Diet.
Add WaterRefills the water bowl.
Pet / Play / HugInteraction that increases affection. Play also uses energy.
Wash / Clean EarsRestores cleanliness. Washing slightly reduces affection (cats dislike water).
Vet VisitSignificantly restores health. Use when cat is sick.
VaccinatePreventive health boost. Increases illness resistance.
Take Out / Stay HomeTake the cat outdoors (boosts affection, but gets dirty) or keep it inside.

System-Calculated Results

Stats change over time based on the cat's personality and player actions. Health is calculated from other stats — if fullness, water, or cleanliness drop too low, health degrades. If the cat is well-fed, hydrated, and clean, health slowly recovers.

Feedback Translation

Sickness SignalHealth < 30Room light dims (visual). Cat stops moving and stays silent. Urgent vet visit needed.
Playfulness SignalNo interaction for 8+ secondsSoft glow highlights the cat. Signals the cat wants attention. Boosts affection when addressed.

Success & Failure Conditions

ConditionTrigger
SuccessCat reaches age 15 years with health > 0 and affection > 0.
Failure — DeathHealth drops to 0 (from illness, hunger, or dehydration).
Failure — AbandonedAffection drops to 0 (cat feels neglected and runs away).

Just-Right Ranges

Too little food: the cat grows hungry easily and risks poor health.
Too much food: the cat feels uncomfortable and may get ill.
The player must find and maintain the right balance for each cat.

Challenge Presets

Each preset changes system variables and relationships, not just visual decoration:

1. Mimi — Balanced House Cat

  • Affection rate: 1.0× (normal growth from interaction)
  • Fullness decay: 1.0× (standard hunger rate)
  • Food preference: Accepts any food type
  • Interaction tolerance: Unlimited (won't get overwhelmed)
  • Illness resistance: 1.0× (normal immunity)
  • Starting stats: Health 80, Affection 50, Energy 70

2. Shadow — Formerly Stray

  • Affection rate: 0.5× (grows HALF as fast — needs patience)
  • Depression rate: 1.2× (loses affection faster when neglected)
  • Food preference: Sensitive stomach — wet food causes health damage. Must use Special Diet.
  • Interaction tolerance: 3 (too many rapid interactions → gets scared → affection drops)
  • Illness resistance: 0.8× (weaker immune system)
  • Starting stats: Health 65, Affection 15, Energy 60

3. Bounce — High Energy

  • Affection rate: 1.3× (extra affection from play specifically)
  • Fullness decay: 1.6× (burns food 60% faster — needs frequent feeding)
  • Energy decay: 1.5× (uses energy quickly)
  • Depression rate: 1.5× (gets depressed fast without attention)
  • Food preference: Accepts any food type
  • Illness resistance: 1.1× (slightly stronger immunity)
  • Starting stats: Health 85, Affection 40, Energy 90

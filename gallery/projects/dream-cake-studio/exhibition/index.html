Dream Cake STUDIO

Bake a cake by learning to adapt — not just following a recipe.

Game Idea

I once wanted to make a hand-made birthday cake for my sister and realized it's far more than following online tutorials. Temperature, ingredients, and tools all matter — and they interact.

Core Learning Shift: Beginners strictly follow fixed recipes, while experts adapt heat, proportions, and cream texture based on on-site conditions.

Domain Knowledge

Domain: Baking

Novice misconception: Beginners think strictly following online tutorial steps is enough.

Expert judgment: Small deviations in oven temperature or ingredient ratios greatly affect the cake's texture and shape. Experts read batter condition, choose proper oven temperature, and plan baking time.

System Design

Observe: Oven temperature, cake rising state, surface color, cream texture, baking time.

Act: Adjust oven temperature, alter batter proportion, add decorative cream, adjust baking duration.

Feedback: Cake cracks → temp too high. Overflow → temp too high + thin batter. Stops rising → oven opened too early.

How to Play (Top-Down View)

↑ or W — Increase Knee Bend    ↓ or S — Decrease Knee Bend    ← or A — Slide LEFT    → or D — Slide RIGHT    Space — Start    P — Pause    R — Restart    1 2 3 — Switch levels

⚠ At game start, your knees are straight (0°) — you can't stand on ice! Use ↑/↓ arrows or the slider to adjust your Knee Bend Angle and find balance. You won't fall during this initial phase — take your time!

Once skating, bending too far (170°+) will make you fall! Stay within the red track boundaries! Level 1: Wide straight track — dodge the center cones! Hit one = fall, miss one = Game Over. Level 2: S-curve track + center cones — hitting the wall = instant Game Over! Level 3: Pure S-curve, no cones — wall = death! + 20s timer! More knee bend = slower speed!

Minimap: Side view in bottom-right shows your knee bend posture and upper body forward lean.

Level 1: Wide straight track, dodge center cones  |  Level 2: Wide S-curve + center cones — wall = death!  |  Level 3: S-curve, tight turns — wall = death! + 20s! Use knee bend to control speed!

Tip: Keep your knee bend around 70-110° for best stability. The farther from 90°, the wobblier you get! More knee bend = slower, less bend = faster!

Speed Control: Use , / . keys (or < / >) to slow down or speed up — adjust your pace to handle sharp turns!

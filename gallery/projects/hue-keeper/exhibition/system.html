Challenge Presets

Each level changes system variables and relationships, not just visual decoration.

Level 1

Matching Only

Fresco needsRed, Blue, Yellow
Flowers provideRed, Blue, Yellow
Fade rate0 (no fading)
MonstersNone
Mixing requiredNo
Level 2

Mixing + Fading

Fresco needsGreen, Orange, Purple, Green, Orange (5 pieces)
Flowers provideRed, Blue, Yellow, Red
Fade rate1.67%/sec (60s to fully fade, per piece)
MonstersNone
Mixing requiredYes (at fountain)
Level 3

Conflict + Survival

Fresco needsGreen, Red, Orange, Blue, Purple, Yellow (6 pieces)
Flowers provideRed, Blue, Yellow, Green, Orange (5 — Purple must be mixed!)
Fade rate1.67%/sec (60s to fully fade, per piece)
MonstersPool of 3, spawn every 5s from any edge (cap shrinks as you defeat them)
Monster speedFast — must attack to clear paths
Monster weaknessComplementary color
Win requirementDefeat ≥2 monsters AND fill all fresco pieces

Success & Failure Conditions

✅ Success

All broken areas on the fresco are filled with matching colors and at least 2 monsters have been defeated using complementary colors (Level 3).

❌ Failure (Level 3)

Pixie is hit by a monster. The game ends immediately with a "Game Over" screen.

❌ Failure (Levels 2–3)

Each splattered color fades individually over 60 seconds. If all fresco pieces fade back to zero after the player has filled at least one, the level fails and must be restarted.

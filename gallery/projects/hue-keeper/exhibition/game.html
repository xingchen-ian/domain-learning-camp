Hue Keeper

Restore the fresco. Mix colors at the fountain. Defeat monsters with complementary colors.

W A S D or ← ↑ ↓ → Move Pixie
1 2 Select palette slot
SPACE Splatter color
R Restart (after win/lose)

How to Play

1. Collect: Walk over a flower to collect its color into your palette (2 slots).
2. Switch: Press 1 or 2 to change which palette slot is active.
3. Mix: Fill both palette slots with primary colors, then walk over the fountain to mix them into a secondary color.
4. Restore: Press SPACE near the fresco to splatter your active color. Match the required color to fill that piece.
5. Defeat (L3): Press SPACE near a monster to attack. Use the complementary color (opposite on the color wheel) to defeat it. The same color makes it stronger!
6. Win: Fill all fresco pieces. Don't let monsters touch you (L3). Don't let the fresco fade away (L2–3).

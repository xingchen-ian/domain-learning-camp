Ski Mountain

Game Idea

You ski down a mountain — but it's not about going fast. Beginners think skiing means pointing straight downhill. Experts know that real skiing is route planning and S-turns to control speed.

Your goal: reach the bottom of the mountain alive. Go too fast straight down and the screen shakes, warning you to carve an S-turn. Hit a rock or tree and you lose health. Stop for 5 seconds and it's over.

Core Learning Shift

Novice misconception: skiing is just a sport that goes straight down. The faster you go, the better.

Expert judgment: you need to plan where you're going and when to turn. S-turns slow you down. Speed control is the real skill. Knowing your level and staying calm matters more than going fast.

Quick Start

A/D — Carve left/right (S-turns slow you down)
W — Lean forward (speed up)
S — Lean backward (brake)
Space — Jump (clears trees, NOT rocks)

Play the Game

Click the game screen and press START. Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move.

Controls

W A S D Move (or Arrow Keys)
F Toggle Flashlight
P / ESC Pause / Resume
Click Click buttons on screen

How to Play

Objective

Navigate through the cave to find the missing explorer (red SOS beacon) and reach the exit (green glowing tile). You must find the explorer before you can escape on Levels 2 and 3.

Survival Tips

  • Follow footprints — they mark safer paths through the cave.
  • Conserve battery — turn off your flashlight when you don't need it. The battery drains faster while moving.
  • Watch for hazards — bats (fluttering shapes), water (blue shimmer), and traps (subtle cracks) all damage your health.
  • Collect supplies — batteries (yellow) restore power; medkits (white with red cross) restore health.
  • Listen for rumbles — in Level 3, the screen will shake and dust will fall when the ceiling is unstable. Keep moving!
  • Don't rush — observe your surroundings before choosing a path. Instinct leads to traps; careful observation leads to success.

Game Over Conditions

  • Time runs out
  • Battery reaches zero and you get lost in darkness
  • Health reaches zero from hazards

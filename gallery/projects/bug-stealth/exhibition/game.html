★ How to Win

Goal: Grow the flycatcher plant to full size, then climb it to escape over the wall!

  1. Pick up items — walk near water bottles and fertilizer bags, press E to grab them.
  2. Feed the plant — stand next to the flycatcher, press E to place items on it.
  3. Aim sunlight (L2/L3 only) — drag mirrors with the mouse to bounce the sun beam onto the plant.
  4. Climb & escape! — when the plant is fully grown (all items delivered + sunlight), stand next to it and hold W / ↑ to climb up and win!

⚠ Avoid the insects! If they spot you, hide in shadows (press Q). Don't let them touch you or you lose!

Bonus: a fully grown flytrap lures and eats nearby insects — grow it early to clear the path!

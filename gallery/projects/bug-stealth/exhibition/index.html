Bug Stealth

A dark miniature-garden stealth game about tending a flycatcher — sneak past insects, bring water and fertilizer, and bounce sunlight through mirrors so the plant grows tall enough to climb to the wall top.

Play the game → Read the domain idea

At a glance

Domain

Plant care — what a pitcher plant / flycatcher really needs to survive: sunlight, soil (fertilizer) and water, while it also preys on insects.

Core learning shift

Beginners only focus on dodging the insects and forget the plant. Under insect pressure the player learns to also watch light paths, supply locations and timing.

Player goal

Place the required supplies on the plant, keep the flycatcher alive, and climb the fully-grown plant to the wall top — without ever being touched by an insect.

Three tiers

L1 needs only water. L2 adds fertilizer. L3 (hard) also demands a valid mirror sunlight reflection path — multi-mirror, multi-insect, lots of barriers.

Summer Camp · Game Analysis

INSIDE 设计分析演示 INSIDE Design Analysis Demo

你刚刚体验了一段游戏。现在把它拆成情感处境、玩家学习、系统反馈、挑战空间和最小原型。

You have just experienced a short game segment. Now break it into emotional situation, player learning, system feedback, challenge space, and minimal prototype.

核心定位：不是模仿 INSIDE 的题材或美术，而是观察它如何让玩家在压力中读懂一个世界。

Core position: do not copy INSIDE's theme or visual style. Learn how it teaches players to read a world under pressure.

Analysis Task

你的分析任务 Your Analysis Task

  1. 回想刚才的 INSIDE 片段：森林、灯光、追捕者、狗、阴影、掩体、跳跃和逃跑。
  2. 先不要急着解释剧情。先记录：我看见什么、做了什么、何时失败、失败后学会什么。
  3. 用接下来的 12 个问题分析这个片段如何运作。
  4. 最后把这种分析方法迁移到你自己的项目：把真实经验或领域学习过程转成可玩的系统。
  1. Recall the INSIDE segment: forest, lights, pursuers, dogs, shadows, cover, jumping, and escape.
  2. Do not rush to explain the story. First note: what I saw, what I did, when I failed, and what I learned after failure.
  3. Use the next 12 questions to analyze how this segment works.
  4. Then transfer this method to your own project: turn lived experience or domain learning into a playable system.

分析重点不是“这个游戏讲了什么故事”，而是“它如何让你在压力中学会读懂这个世界”。

The focus is not "what story does this game tell?" but "how does it make you learn to read this world under pressure?"

Part 1 · Emotional Situation

问题 1-3：先问情感处境 Questions 1-3: Start with Emotional Situation

原因：先确定游戏想让玩家处在什么情感结构里。否则很容易只分析操作、关卡或美术。

Reason: first identify the emotional structure the game puts the player in. Otherwise it is easy to only analyze controls, levels, or visual style.

1
这个片段让玩家处在什么处境中？ What situation does this segment put the player in? 强大还是弱小？自由还是受控？安全还是暴露？ Powerful or weak? Free or controlled? Safe or exposed?
2
这个处境带来的核心情感是什么？ What core emotion does this situation create? 恐惧、紧张、压迫、孤独、无助、好奇，哪个最强？它如何变化？ Fear, tension, pressure, loneliness, helplessness, curiosity: which is strongest? How does it change?
3
游戏如何不靠文字，而让玩家感受到这些情感？ How does the game make the player feel this without explaining it in text? 画面、声音、空间、规则和失败方式分别有什么作用？ What roles do visuals, sound, space, rules, and failure play?

Part 1 · Demo Answer

INSIDE 示例回答：情感处境 INSIDE Demo Answer: Emotional Situation

处境结构：弱小身体 + 敌意环境 + 信息不足 + 必须前进

Situation structure: weak body + hostile environment + incomplete information + forced forward movement

核心情感Core Emotion

不是单纯恐怖，而是“被追捕的压迫感”和“无助中的紧张判断”。

Not just horror, but the pressure of being hunted and the tense judgment inside helplessness.

情感轨迹Emotional Path

不确定 → 警觉 → 被追赶的紧张 → 失败后的惊吓 → 学会观察后的短暂安全感。

Uncertainty → alertness → chase tension → shock after failure → brief safety after learning to observe.

设计元素Design Element 情感作用Emotional Role
黑暗森林和远景空间Dark forest and distant space 孤立、方向不明Isolation and unclear direction
车辆灯光Vehicle lights 被监视、被发现的压力Pressure of being watched and discovered
狗叫声和奔跑声Dog barks and running sounds 让追捕压力变得身体化Makes pursuit pressure bodily and immediate

Part 2 · Player Learning Loop

问题 4-6：玩家如何从不会到会？ Questions 4-6: How Does the Player Learn?

原因：这里借用 Domain Learning。你要看玩家如何从“不会读这个世界”变成“会根据环境信号行动”。

Reason: this is where Domain Learning helps. You examine how the player moves from not reading the world to acting through environmental signals.

4
玩家一开始最容易误解什么？ What does the player most easily misunderstand at first? 是不是以为只要一直向右跑、动作快一点就可以？ Do they think they only need to keep running right and move faster?
5
玩家失败后开始观察什么？ What does the player start observing after failure? 灯光、狗的距离、敌人视线、掩体、跳跃距离、声音节奏。 Light, dog distance, enemy vision, cover, jump distance, sound rhythm.
6
玩家形成的核心学习循环是什么？ What is the player's core learning loop? 观察什么 → 判断什么 → 做什么 → 得到什么反馈 → 下次如何调整。 What to observe → what to judge → what to do → what feedback appears → how to adjust next time.

Part 2 · Demo Answer

INSIDE 示例回答：学习循环 INSIDE Demo Answer: Learning Loop

新手误解Beginner Misunderstanding

“这是一个向右跑的平台游戏，只要跑快一点就能逃走。”

"This is a side-scrolling platform game. If I run fast enough, I can escape."

失败后的转变Shift After Failure

玩家从“操作角色”转向“读取环境”：灯光何时扫来、狗离自己多远、哪里有阴影、什么时候停或冲。

The player shifts from controlling a character to reading the environment: when light sweeps over, how close the dog is, where shadows are, when to stop or sprint.

观察灯光 / 敌人 / 狗 / 掩体Observe light / enemies / dogs / cover
↓
判断现在跑、停还是躲Judge whether to run, stop, or hide
↓
看到被发现、被追上、死亡或成功通过See detection, being caught, death, or successful passage
↓
调整行动时机和路线，更会读这个危险世界Adjust timing and route, and become better at reading this dangerous world

Part 3 · Feedback and Challenge Space

问题 7-9：反馈与挑战空间 Questions 7-9: Feedback and Challenge Space

原因：这是系统分析的核心。挑战不是随机灵感，而是由少数因素不断组合。

Reason: this is the core of system analysis. Challenges do not come from random inspiration; they emerge from combinations of key factors.

7
游戏给玩家的关键反馈有哪些？ What key feedback does the game give the player? 视觉反馈、声音反馈、失败反馈分别是什么？ What are the visual, audio, and failure feedback signals?
8
这个片段的挑战因素有哪些？ What challenge factors exist in this segment? 灯光范围、敌人视线、狗速度、掩体位置、距离、跳跃时机、声音提示。 Light range, enemy vision, dog speed, cover position, distance, jump timing, sound cues.
9
这些因素如何组合成从简单到复杂的挑战？ How do these factors combine into simple-to-complex challenges? 先单独出现什么？后来叠加什么？叠加后策略如何改变？ What appears alone first? What is layered later? How must strategy change?

These feedback signals barely use scores. The player learns through visuals, sound, action results, and failure.

Part 4 · Minimal Prototype Transfer

问题 10-12：提取一个最小原型 Questions 10-12: Extract a Minimal Prototype

原因：分析成熟游戏不是为了复制它，而是为了提取一个可学习、可实现的小系统。

Reason: analyzing a mature game is not about copying it. It is about extracting a small system that can be learned and built.

10
如果只保留一个 30 秒核心循环，玩家能做什么？ If we keep only one 30-second core loop, what can the player do?
11
这个小原型必须保留哪些反馈，才能保留原来的情感体验？ What feedback must this small prototype keep to preserve the original emotional experience?
12
设计 2-3 个递进挑战，难度如何变化？ Design 2-3 progressive challenges. How does difficulty change?

Advanced Questions

进一步追问 Further Questions

情感路径补充Emotional Path

  • 情感高峰在哪里？
  • 玩家什么时候最无助？
  • 短暂掌控感是否消除压迫？
  • Where is the emotional peak?
  • When does the player feel most helpless?
  • Does brief control remove the pressure?

系统分析补充System Analysis

  • 哪些变量可以实现？
  • 哪些状态最重要？
  • 失败后能否知道错在哪里？
  • What variables could implement this?
  • Which states matter most?
  • Can players understand why they failed?

AI 原型补充AI Prototype

  • 提示词必须说明哪些系统结构？
  • AI 可能把它变成什么普通小游戏？
  • 你会拒绝哪些 AI 建议？
  • What system structure must the prompt include?
  • How might AI turn it into a generic game?
  • Which AI suggestions should you reject?

这些问题不一定全部回答。选择最能帮助你理解这个片段的问题，并把答案连接到你的原型设计。

You do not need to answer all of these. Choose the questions that best help you understand this segment, then connect your answers to your prototype design.

From INSIDE to Your Project

从 INSIDE 转向你自己的游戏设计 From INSIDE to Your Own Game Design

分析 INSIDE 不是为了复制它，而是为了学会选择自己的设计入口。你的项目可以从情感经验出发，也可以从领域学习出发。

Analyzing INSIDE is not about copying it. It helps you choose your own design entry point. Your project can start from emotional experience or from domain learning.

情感导向Emotion-Led

  • 我想让玩家感受到什么？
  • 什么生活处境会产生这种情感？
  • 哪些规则、空间和反馈能让玩家感到它？
  • What do I want the player to feel?
  • What lived situation creates this emotion?
  • What rules, spaces, and feedback can make the player feel it?

领域学习导向Domain-Learning-Led

  • 我想转化哪个领域？
  • 新手最容易误解什么？
  • 玩家要观察、判断、行动、反馈和调整什么？
  • What domain do I want to transform?
  • What do beginners misunderstand?
  • What must the player observe, judge, do, receive feedback on, and adjust?

整合方式How They Connect

  • 情感回答：为什么这个游戏值得被设计？
  • 领域学习回答：玩家如何一步步学会？
  • 挑战空间回答：难度如何由系统因素生成？
  • Emotion answers: why is this game worth designing?
  • Domain learning answers: how does the player learn step by step?
  • Challenge space answers: how does difficulty emerge from system factors?

下一步：用这两个入口，开始回答你自己的游戏设计问题清单。

Next step: use these two entry points to answer your own game design question list.

你是创作者You Are the Creator

This is not a coding camp, and we do not hand design or development over to AI.

You choose the experience, the system, and the purpose. AI agents are your assistants — they build, document, and help you iterate.

人与 AIHuman & AI

创作者The creator

  • 选择领域与真实经验Choose domain and real-life experience
  • 把要素变成数据、规则与反馈Turn elements into data, rules, feedback
  • 描述并可视化系统Describe and visualize the system
  • 与 AI 迭代——保持批判性审视Iterate with AI — review critically
  • 反思决策与协作Reflect on decisions and collaboration

AI 助手AI assistants

  • 加速构建与文档Accelerate building and documentation
  • 协助快速实现与迭代Help implement and iterate quickly
  • 提供备选方案——不是替你下判断Suggest alternatives — not judgment
  • 无法决定这个游戏为何值得做Cannot decide why the game is worth making

AI 可以加速设计与开发，但不能替你决定这个游戏为什么值得被做出来。 AI can speed up design and development, but it cannot decide why this game is worth making.
